## Introduction

**PhotoSync** is your private cloud, simple and secure.

With PhotoSync Desktop, you can easily set up a local file transfer service on your computer without relying on third-party cloud storage. Used in conjunction with the PhotoSync App on your mobile device, it enables high-speed backup and management of photos and videos, keeping your data completely in your control.
