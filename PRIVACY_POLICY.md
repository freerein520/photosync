# Privacy Policy for PhotoSync

Last updated: 2026-03-19

PhotoSync respects your privacy. This policy explains how the app handles data.

## 1. Data We Collect

PhotoSync does not require account registration and does not collect personal profile information such as name, phone number, or identity number.

To provide sync features, the app may process:

- device-local photos and videos selected by the user,
- basic app operation logs (for troubleshooting),
- local network connection information required to connect your own devices.

## 2. How Data Is Used

Processed data is used only for:

- transferring files between your devices,
- displaying sync status and history in the app,
- diagnosing technical problems when needed.

## 3. Data Transmission And Storage

- File transfer is designed for local network communication between your devices.
- PhotoSync does not require uploading your photos and videos to PhotoSync-owned cloud servers for the core sync flow.
- Data is stored on your own devices unless you explicitly export or share it.

## 4. Permissions

Depending on Android version and features used, the app may request permissions such as:

- media/photos access,
- local network access.

Permissions are used only to enable requested app functions.

## 5. Third-Party Services

If third-party libraries/services are used in future releases, their data handling terms will be disclosed in app updates and release notes.

## 6. Children

PhotoSync is not directed to children under the age defined by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided data improperly, contact us and we will handle it promptly.

## 7. Your Rights

You may contact us to request help regarding:

- data access questions,
- deletion-related support,
- privacy concerns.
